Get Earthed!

We are on a mission to heal our connection to nature through movement and meditation. We inspire people to harmonize with the cycles of life and awaken a more connected Earth together.

Humans evolved in connection with the Earth’s healing energy. Today, the modern world has increasingly separated us from the primordial flow of Earth’s electrons. This disconnect contributes to major physiological dysfunctions and a state of ill health. A review of the health implications of reconnecting the human body to the Earth’s electrons in the Journal of Environmental and Public Health found many beneficial physiological changes such as improved inflammation disorders, reduced chronic pain, deeper sleep, increased vitality, lower stress levels, relief of muscle tension and headache, improved blood pressure, and stabilized biological rhythms. Through embodying true connection and oneness with all life on Earth, we will come to know inner peace and understand the importance of conserving the natural world.

♬ Dance with nature - Cam & Eli 🌞

In 2019, Cam and Eli danced into each other’s lives in Bali, Indonesia. Cam is from Hong Kong and Eli is from Brooklyn, New York. Cam was working in communications and conservation science for the Coral Triangle Center, a local non-profit organization that inspires people to save the oceans. Eli was engaged with projects through his innovation lab, Change Init, and the lead instructor at Le Wagon Bali, a global coding bootcamp. Together, they hosted silent sunset dances on the beaches of Bali with mindfully curated playlists, empowering people to embrace and connect with Mother Earth — bringing Groove with Gaia to life.

Cam holds a B.A. in Earth and Oceanographic Science and Environmental Studies, and is currently pursuing a M.S. in Natural Resources and Environmental Management at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. Over the years, she has pursued research in biological oceanography at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) and several academic institutions. Recently, her research interests have shifted towards understanding and developing social-ecological resilient coral reef management strategies for vulnerable island nations.

Eli is a multi-disciplinary entrepreneur crossing the inevitabilities of technology with the insights of ancestral knowledge. He was first an innovation consultant at a foresight and futures consultancy, then led corporate innovation at Southeast Asia’s first startup ecosystem prior to leaving job life to discover freedom. He has since lived and worked across the globe bringing whole-systems thinking to for-purpose endeavors surrounding education, empowerment, and ecology. He combines the technical, creative, and strategic to sculpt and drive missions that raise vibration.
